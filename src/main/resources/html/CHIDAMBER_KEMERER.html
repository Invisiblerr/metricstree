Chidamber-Kemerer Metrics Set

In 1991, Chidamber and Kemerer proposed a first version of set of metrics and later the definition of some of them were improved and presented in 1994 [1]. The Chidamber & Kemerer metrics set consists of 6 metrics calculated for each class: WMC, DIT, NOC, CBO, RFC and LCOM.

Objective of CK metrics were defined to measure design complexity in relation to their impact on external quality attributes such as maintainability, reusability, etc.

[1] S. R. Chidamber and C. F. Kemerer. A Metrics Suite for Object Oriented Design. In IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering, volume 20 (6), pages 476-493, June 1994.
